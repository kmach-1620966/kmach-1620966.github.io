History

Originally founded in 1990 by other individuals, the RSO was shutdown because the club became inactive as the president was unable to find someone to take his position.

Back and better than ever, we have a strong foundation of over one hundred members with new officers who are elected every year by the members of the RSO. We typically have multiple positions open in the beginning of the quarter (before November). If interested, please click on the link below.

Learn More

What We Do

We love to create event pages on Facebook and take trips to local locations like Downtown Seattle to do group shoots. People tend to pair off or travel in groups and it is a great way to get to know each other and learn about other people's styles when it comes to media.

There will be tons of opportunites to showcase your work in various ways such as the Seattle Film Festival and Seattle Photography competition. We tend to do well in these competitions and would love to have more members entering in these projects together or individual.
